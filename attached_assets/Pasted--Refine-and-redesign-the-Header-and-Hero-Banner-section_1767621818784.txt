“Refine and redesign the Header and Hero (Banner) sections to be clearly distinct, visually separated, and mobile-safe. Currently, the header and banner are visually smudged together, and on mobile the banner overlaps and hides the logo. Fix this by introducing clear spatial separation, layering, and z-index control.

Header Requirements:

Make the header a clearly defined, standalone component with a solid or semi-transparent background (not blending into the hero).

Ensure the logo is always fully visible on mobile and desktop.

Add sufficient vertical padding and height consistency across breakpoints.

Implement a sticky header that transitions cleanly on scroll (e.g., subtle shadow or background color change).

Ensure proper z-index so the header always stays above the hero/banner.

Hero / Banner Requirements:

Visually separate the hero section from the header using spacing, a divider, or background contrast.

Avoid background elements, gradients, or animations bleeding into the header area.

On mobile, reduce hero height and reposition text so it does not collide with the header.

Ensure hero typography scales correctly and remains readable on smaller screens.

Mobile-Specific Fixes:

Explicitly handle mobile breakpoints so header and banner do not overlap.

Prevent absolute-positioned hero elements from covering the logo.

Test and optimize for small screen heights.

Footer Enhancement:

Under the ‘About’ section in the footer, add two new links: ‘Team’ and ‘Careers’.

Keep these links visually secondary but clearly discoverable.

Maintain consistency with the overall typography and color system.

Color Palette (Strictly Apply):

Primary: #2b204c (Dark Indigo)

Secondary: #8b68f6 (Vivid Violet)

Accent: #952828 (Saffron Red)

Neutral: #F9FAFB (Light Gray)

Text: #111827 (Dark Gray)

Use Dark Indigo for header background or text, Vivid Violet for gradients or highlights, Saffron Red sparingly for CTAs or emphasis.

Interaction & Animation Improvements (Replace Hover-Only Behavior):

The ‘News & Insights’ and ‘Our Portfolio’ sections currently rely on hover-based reveals, which do not translate well to mobile.

Replace hover-only interactions with scroll-triggered or tap-friendly animations.

Suggested alternatives:

Scroll-based reveal animations (fade-up, slide-in, or scale-in).

Persistent preview cards with expandable details on click/tap.

Subtle auto-animated emphasis (e.g., slow pulse, border glow, or gradient shift).

Ensure animations feel premium, restrained, and VC-grade—not playful or gimmicky.

Overall Design Intent:

The result should feel clean, intentional, and confident.

Header and hero must feel architecturally separated.

Mobile experience should feel designed-first, not adapted.

The site should reflect a serious venture builder with strong execution credibility.”**
