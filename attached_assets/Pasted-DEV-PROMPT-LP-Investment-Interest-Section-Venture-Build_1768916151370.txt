DEV PROMPT — LP Investment Interest Section (Venture Builder)

Add a compact, high-impact section on the Venture Builder page inviting Limited Partners (LPs) and strategic investors to express interest in investing in the Venture Builder. This section should communicate urgency, scarcity, and strong financial upside while maintaining a professional, institutional tone.

1️⃣ Section Placement

Place the section:

After Current Builds

Or just before the final page CTA

Ensure it feels intentional, not promotional

2️⃣ Messaging Direction (Tone & Intent)

Convey:

Limited allocation

Active deal flow

Early entry advantage

Avoid:

Overpromising returns

Aggressive marketing language

Sample headline style (developer to refine copy):

“Now Raising: Venture Builder Allocation”

“Partner With the Venture Builder Shaping the Next Wave of Deep Tech”

Supporting message should emphasize:

Proven venture-building capability

Active portfolio creation

Institutional partnerships

Early LP advantage before close

3️⃣ Visual Treatment

Compact highlighted block:

Soft accent background

Thin border or subtle gradient

Clear visual separation from content sections

No large hero or banner

4️⃣ LP Interest Form (Attached to CTA)

CTA Button:

Text options:

Express LP Interest

Invest in the Venture Builder

Request LP Information

Form Behavior:

Opens in:

Modal / popup or

Inline expandable form (preferred for credibility)

Form Fields (Minimal & Serious):

Full Name

Organization / Fund Name

Email

Investment Interest Range (dropdown)

Geography

Optional note

5️⃣ Urgency & FOMO Elements (Subtle)

Small supporting cues:

“Limited LP slots available”

“First close approaching”

“By invitation and referral”

Optional micro-badge:

“Currently Raising”

6️⃣ Trust Signals (Optional but Recommended)

Small line below form:

“Selective LP onboarding”

“Institutional & strategic partners only”

No financial promises or numbers

7️⃣ Mobile Experience

CTA remains prominent but non-intrusive

Modal must be:

Fast

Minimal scrolling

Easy to close

🎯 Goal

Make qualified LPs feel they are early, selected, and at risk of missing access—while reinforcing Risin Ventures as a disciplined, execution-driven venture builder.
