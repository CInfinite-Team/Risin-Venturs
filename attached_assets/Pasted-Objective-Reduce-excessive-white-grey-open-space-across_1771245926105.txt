Objective

Reduce excessive white/grey/open space across homepage to:

Make homepage feel tighter and more structured

Reduce scroll length

Improve visual density

Maintain premium feel (do NOT overcrowd)

Target: Reduce vertical length by ~20–25%.

✅ STAGE 1 – SECTION-LEVEL SPACING REDUCTION
Block 1: Reduce Section Padding

Current sections likely have excessive padding (example: 120px+).

Update all sections:

Desktop:

Top padding: 60–80px max

Bottom padding: 60–80px max

Tablet:

50–60px

Mobile:

40–50px

Ensure consistency across all sections.

Block 2: Reduce Gap Between Sections

Remove excessive margin between:

Partners → Metrics

Metrics → Venture Building

Programs → Portfolio

Portfolio → Case Studies

Use:

40–60px spacing max between major blocks.

Avoid large empty transitional gaps.

✅ STAGE 2 – WITHIN-SECTION SPACING FIX
Block 3: Tighten Heading to Content Gap

Reduce space between:

Section heading → paragraph

Paragraph → cards

Heading → logos

Heading → metrics

Target:

16–24px between heading and text

24–32px between text and grid/cards

Remove oversized 60–80px gaps.

Block 4: Reduce Card Padding

If cards feel oversized:

Reduce internal padding by 15–20%

Maintain breathing space

Avoid massive top-heavy spacing inside cards

Premium ≠ excessive padding.

✅ STAGE 3 – HERO SECTION ADJUSTMENT
Block 5: First Fold Optimization

Reduce:

Large top padding in hero

Large empty space below CTA

Ensure:

Hero content sits closer to center

Logos section begins sooner (above fold if possible on large screens)

✅ STAGE 4 – FOOTER & CTA TIGHTENING
Block 6:

Reduce oversized padding in:

Final CTA section

Footer

Maintain clarity but remove excess vertical stretch.

🚫 DO NOT:

Remove spacing so much that sections feel crowded

Stack elements too tightly

Remove visual hierarchy

Break mobile spacing logic

We are compressing intelligently — not shrinking blindly.
